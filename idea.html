Reduce Use of Single-Use Plastics is the easiest way reducing your own use of single-use plastics.plastic bags, bottles, cups, take-out containers, and any other plastic items that are used once and then discarded. single-use plastic items, help businesses by letting them know that you would like them to offer alternatives.


Microbeads(tiny plastic particles) have become a growing source of ocean plastic pollution. Microbeads are found in some face scrubs, toothpastes, and they readily enter our oceans and waterways through our systems, and affect hundreds of ocean animals. Avoid using microbeads product by looking for “polythelene” and “polypropylene” on the ingredient labels of your life products.
